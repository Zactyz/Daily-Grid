Privacy Policy

Last updated December 28, 2025

This Privacy Notice for Zachary Zimmerman ("we," "us," or "our"), describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including when you:

  • Download and use our mobile application (Daily Grid), or any other application of ours that links to this Privacy Notice
  • Use Daily Grid. Daily Grid is a mobile logic puzzle game that delivers a new handcrafted grid puzzle each day. Users solve each puzzle by placing virtual ornaments on a grid according to simple logical rules, with no guessing required. As users complete puzzles, they unlock cosmetic decorations to personalize an in-app scene. Daily Grid is designed for calm, focused play, with an optional progression of seasonal themes and personalization elements.
  • Engage with us in other related ways, including any marketing or events

Questions or concerns? Reading this Privacy Notice will help you understand your privacy rights and choices. We are responsible for making decisions about how your personal information is processed. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services. If you still have any questions or concerns, please contact us at support@dailygrid.app.
