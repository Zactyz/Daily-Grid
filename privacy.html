PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated December 28, 2025

This Privacy Notice for Zachary Zimmerman ("we," "us," or "our"), describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including when you download and use our mobile application (Daily Grid).
Daily Grid is a mobile logic puzzle game that delivers a new handcrafted grid puzzle each day. Users solve each puzzle by placing virtual ornaments on a grid according to simple logical rules, with no guessing required. As users complete puzzles, they unlock cosmetic decorations to personalize an in-app scene. Daily Grid is designed for calm, focused play, with an optional progression of seasonal themes and personalization elements.
Daily Grid does not require user accounts, email login, or personal information to play the core game. The puzzle solving and decoration features are fully functional offline once content is downloaded to the device.
The app may offer optional in-app purchases through Apple’s App Store, including a premium subscription that unlocks additional features. Payment processing is handled by Apple via the App Store, and Daily Grid does not directly collect or store users’ financial or payment card information.

SUMMARY OF KEY POINTS

What personal information do we process? When you visit, use, or navigate our Services, we may process personal information depending on how you interact with us and the Services. We do not process sensitive personal information.

Do we collect any information from third parties? We do not collect any information from third parties.

How do we process your information? We process your information to provide, improve, and administer our Services, communicate with you, for security and fraud prevention, and to comply with law.
