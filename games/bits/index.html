How to Play

Every row and column must contain exactly three zeroes and three ones. No three identical bits may touch horizontally or vertically. The server gives you a handful of clues to guide the rest.

  • Tap a cell to cycle between 0, 1, and blank.
  • Clues are locked and glow softly.
  • Fill the entire grid correctly to reveal the leaderboard.
