How to Play
  • 1Each island shows how many bridges must connect to it.
  • 2Connect islands in straight lines, horizontally or vertically.
  • 3No bridges can cross, and no island can have more than two bridges between a pair.
  • 4All islands must be in one connected network.

Tip: Click an island, then another to add bridges. Click again to cycle 1, 2, or none.
