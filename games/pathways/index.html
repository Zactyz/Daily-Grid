How to Play
  • 1 Tap a colored dot to select that color
  • 2 Drag to connect the matching colored dots
  • 3 Lines can only go horizontally or vertically (no diagonals)
  • 4 Lines cannot cross or overlap
  • 5 Fill every cell to complete the puzzle

Tip: Drag backwards on a path to undo moves. Tap different colors to switch between them.
