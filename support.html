Frequently Asked Questions

How do I solve the puzzle?

Place exactly the right number of stars in each region, row, and column. Stars cannot touch each other, not even diagonally!

When is a new puzzle released?

A brand new, handcrafted puzzle is released every single day at midnight.

I missed a day, can I still play it?

Yes! With a premium subscription, you can access the entire archive of previous puzzles.
